Conference Location

Advances in the collective behaviour of complex systems will take place on the Golm campus (Komplex II) of the University of Potsdam. The conference sessions will happen in the lecture halls of the ground floor of the physics building (building 28 near the railway station). The Mensa is located in building 4.
