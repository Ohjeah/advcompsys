Conference Location

Advances in the collective behaviour of complex systems will take place on the Golm campus (Komplex II) of the University of Potsdam.
The conference sessions will happen in the lecture halls of the ground floor of the physics building (building 28 near the railway station).
The Mensa is located in building 4.

City of Potsdam – University campuses and public transport

How to get to Golm

  • by regional trains:

    • RB 21 und RB20 from Potsdam main station

  • by Potsdam city buses:

    • X5 Potsdam Hbf to Bhf Golm, get off at Bhf Golm/Universität
    • 606 Potsdam Hbf to Alt-Golm, get off at Bhf Golm/Universität
    • 605 Potsdam Hbf to Wissenschaftspark Golm, get off at Bhf Golm

  • by car:

    • e.g. A10, exit Potsdam Nord or Leest, go in direction of Golm. Coming from Potsdam go in direction of Eiche/Golm.

The university campus Golm is located opposite the station Golm at the Karl-Liebknecht-Straße.
